Portfolio

MappyHAU

MappyHAU is an application particularly developed for Holy Angel University students and new visitors who are unfamiliar with the whereabouts of the offices, buildings or departments. This app is a map of the university that shows the locations and descriptions of buildings. This app shows the user pictures of each building at Holy Angel University so that they may quickly figure out where they are heading. This app will be incredibly useful for new students or transferees who are unfamiliar with the school.

Hotel DApp

This web application is a decentralized application (dApp) that enhances the hotel booking experience while streamlining operations for hotel management. This system aims to empower hotel owners and customers by decentralizing the reservation process, thereby offering a more secure, transparent, and cost-effective alternative to traditional booking platforms. This dApp is designed to enhance the overall customer experience, enabling secure, efficient bookings while increasing operational efficiency for hotel managers.

Grade Predictor App

This Grade Predictor App is to predict the academic performance of grade 6 students using a multi-level classification system. Moreover, the aim of this is to provide an administrative hub for teachers to early determine students’ academic performance and to adjust teaching methods, visual aids, and other learning materials.
